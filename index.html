about IEDC

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centres (IEDC) are platforms set up with an aim to provide students with an opportunity to experiment and innovate. Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) is a flagship initiative of Kerala Startup Mission to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among the student and academic fraternity in the educational institutions in the State of Kerala and considered as an umbrella programme that would play an instrumental role in fostering innovation culture in Academic institutions. IEDC, MCAS is set up for promoting innovative ideas and to support the students to develop them into solid projects. IEDC MCAS aims in fostering entrepreneurial culture in the college and to provide a platform for the students to pursue entrepreneurial activities. The Main objective is to provide assistance to potential entrepreneurs and innovators. IEDC is planning to conduct activities that would develop entrepreneurial and innovative qualities in students and also make sure to provide common facilities to students working on start-ups.
