About Us

Step into a world of innovation and entrepreneurship at the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) of Malabar College of Advanced Studies (MCAS)! Founded in 2022, we have been a catalyst for fostering a vibrant culture of innovation among our students.

Embrace the spirit of creativity and exploration as IEDC MCAS becomes your dynamic hub for learning, ideation, and growth. Through our engaging workshops, seminars, Hackathons, and Idea Fest competitions, we empower aspiring entrepreneurs to transform their ideas into successful ventures.

Recognized for our commitment to excellence, IEDC MCAS proudly received the State Emerging IEDC award in the 2022-23 academic year. As a premier platform, we provide unparalleled support to incubated start-ups, offering financial aid, access to cutting-edge technologies, and invaluable mentoring.

Join us on this exciting journey of discovery and innovation, where tomorrow's trailblazers are nurtured today. Together, let's build a future filled with transformative ideas and boundless possibilities!

Vision

To provide an incubation space for the students to promote and support student innovations.

Mission

To develop an entrepreneurial culture among the students and to foster the growth of innovation and entrepreneurship among the faculty and students.

Objectives

To provide assistance to potential entrepreneurs and innovators and also make sure to provide common facilities to students working on start-ups.
